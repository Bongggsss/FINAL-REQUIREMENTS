ABOUT US

Founded on a passion for flavor and a love of community,
K D P is more than just a milk tea business, it's a destination for refreshment,
relaxation, and camaraderie. Our journey began with a simple desire to share
the delight of milk tea with others, and it has since blossomed into a
hub of creativity and connection.

MISSION

At K D P, our mission is to craft exceptional milk tea experiences that bring joy, comfort,
and connection to our customers. We are dedicated to using the finest ingredients,
innovative techniques, and personalized service to create beverages that delight the senses
and nourish the soul. With a commitment to sustainability and community, we aim to
not only satisfy cravings but also to make a positive impact on the world around us.
